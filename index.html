I'm Not Letting You Go

In fields of roses, I find you there,
A gentle bloom, beyond compare.
With every petal, soft and true,
My heart whispers, "I belong to you."

Though winds may pull and storms may rise,
I see forever in your eyes.
With every beat, my love will grow,
And I’m not letting you go.

In nights of dark and days of light,
You are my warmth, my heart’s delight.
A vow I make, a promise clear,
Forever held, forever near.

So here I stand, with love to show,
My dear, I’m not letting you go.

Through seasons' change and colors fade,
With hands entwined, our steps are laid.
The paths we walk, both high and low,
Together still, where dreams will flow.

In winter’s chill or summer’s glow,
I feel your presence, steady and slow.
Each moment shared, a bond we sew,
And I'm not letting you go.

You’re in each breath, in every sigh,
The reason stars light up my sky.
Though years may pass, and time may show,
My love is true, and it will grow.

So take my hand, and let us be
Two souls bound, eternally.
No force can break, no tide can throw
The strength within — I won’t let go.
